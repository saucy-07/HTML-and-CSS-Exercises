The Toucan

Toucans are well known for their big, brightly coloured beaks and exceptional beauty. These birds are native to the Neotropics from Southern Mexico through to Northern Argentina.


Toucans are social birds as they normally live in groups of around 20 or more birds at a time. They spend time brawling it out with their beaks and playing 'tag'. These birds nest within tree hollows and will generally produce around 2 to 4 white eggs. The toucan's main source of food are fruits but do eat insects, smaller birds and lizards.
